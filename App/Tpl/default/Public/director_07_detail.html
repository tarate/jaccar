VO THI HUYEN LAN

Born October 16, 1971

Vietnamese Nationality


Mrs. Vo Thi Huyen Lan holds a DEA in Finance from the HEC business school, Paris. Having worked as Chief Accountant of PRESIOZO Vietnam in 1996, she was then the Chief Financial Officer then Deputy General Manager of BOURBON’s Retail in Vietnam by 1998. Mrs. Lan joined JACCAR Vietnam in 2006 as CEO. Currently, she is the Managing Director of JACCAR HOLDINGS, a Board Member of BOURBON and SINOPACIFIC Shipbuilding Group, as well as of other companies in Vietnam (listed below). Vo Thi Huyen Lan became a Board Member of JACCAR in 2009.
