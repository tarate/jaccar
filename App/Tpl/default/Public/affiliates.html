BOURBON

BOURBON offers a comprehensive range of maritime services for offshore oil and gas fields and wind farms, based on a latest-generation fleet. The Group provides a local service through its 26 operating subsidiaries, and guarantees the highest standards of service quality and safety worldwide (including the protection of the French coastline for the French Navy).

Classified by ICB (Industry Classification Benchmark) in the "Oil Services" sector, BOURBON is listed for trading on Euronext Paris, Compartment A, and is included in the Deferred Settlement Service SRD and in the SBF 120, CAC Mid 60 and Dow Jones Stoxx 600 indices.

SAPMER

Created in 1947 and based in Reunion Island, the SAPMER of today conducts two complementary activities in the Territory of the French Southern and Antarctic Lands, or, Terres australes et antarctiques françaises (TAAF).

At Sea: Lobstering and fishing of Yellowfin and Skipjack tuna as well as toothfish – all in the Indian Ocean with onboard deep-freezing at -40°C.

Onshore: Management and constant improvement of all halieutic resources at Mer des Mascareignes factory on Mauritius Island.

For 60 years, the 300-strong team has ceaselessly maintained a deep involvement in the preservation of all aquatic resources.

SAPMER fishes responsibly and benefits from state-of-the-art industrial maritime tools and methods. In April 2010, SAPMER received the honour of a ‘responsible fishing’ mention for its tuna activity, and in August 2011, joined the ‘Dolphin Safe’ program (ensuring the safety of Dolphins and many other marine animals). In addition, SAPMER has maintained a listing on Alternext, Paris since July 8, 2009.

GREENSHIP / EVERGAS

EVERGAS is a leading seaborne transporter of petrochemical gases. The company is based in Copenhagen and employs more than 400 people worldwide.

Its ethylene fleet of 8 ethylene carriers is commercially managed by EVERGAS Solvang Ethylene and its pressurized fleet by GasChem Services.

By January 2013,12 new buildings of 12.000-17.000 m³ will be erected and 6 fully pressurized vessels of 5.000 m³ are under construction and will be delivered during 2012.

GREENSHIP / SETAF

Incorporated in 1968 with the purpose of offering shipping expertise to French companies, the SETAF Group of today is a provider of comprehensive bulk shipping solutions to large industrial customers worldwide.

From ship design to cargo expertise, SETAF Group extends its expertise of seaborne bulk transportation – including operation of a full modern fleet of Supramax bulk carriers, as well as chartering bulk carriers from all sizes up to Panamax.

Today, SETAF-SAGET is still owned by his founder JL Bottaro & Associates, and managed by a board of highly qualified shipping professionals sharing the same commitment to reliability and safety of all services offered.

SINOPACIGFIC SHIPBUILDING GROUP

Sinopacific Shipbuilding Group is a diversified corporation engaged in the design, trade and building of maritime industry vessels and carriers including: bulk carriers, container ships, offshore oil platform supply vessels, as well as gas carrier and liquid cargo vessels.

With 2 shipyards (Zhejiang Shipbuilding Co., Ltd and Yangzhou Dayang Shipbuilding Co., Ltd) and its own Research, Development and Technical Teams, the company manages a market-stronghold.

Through an integrated management system, the group achieves centralized resource management, continual cost reductions and increased production efficiency – a solid foundation to continue its growth in its role as a leading company in China’s ship building industry

PIRIOU

Half a century of experience and continuous growth have helped establish PIRIOU as a leading specialist in the fields of shipbuilding and ship repair for medium sized vessels.

In line with its focus to remain competitive and meet the needs of overseas customers, PIRIOU established 3 production sites between 2001 and 2006: Mauritius, Nigeria and Vietnam, as well as a research department in Poland.

From ship design, to shipbuilding, maintenance and throughout production, the PIRIOU group offers its global customer-base a full spectrum of custom shipbuilding and ship maintenance solutions.

CBo Territoria

As the owner of 3,000 hectares of strategically located land on Reunion Island, CBo Territoria is the leading specialist in property and asset management and development in cooperation with local authorities.

In keeping with its sustainable development policy, the company develops projects that enhance the island’s hospitality sector. By 2020, Beauséjour, a new town of 78 hectares near Saint-Denis (the island’s largest city) will welcome 8,000 inhabitants and provide 2,300 units with nearby businesses, services and equipment as well as a business park covering 82,000 square-meters.

The remarkable size of its operations and the high level of products and services that CBo Territoria provides is how the company maintains such a glowing reputation within this thriving, dynamic market.

HAGL

Joang Anh Gia Lai (HAGL) has one of the most extraordinary private business stories in Vietnam. In 1992, Mr. Doan Nguyen Duc used profits from his small furniture workshop in Gia Lai (in the remote highlands of Vietnam), to invest in real estate amidst a boom across the country.

Today, Mr. Doan Nguyen Duc is among the top 30 most powerful people in business according to the ASEAN (Association of Southeast Asian Nations), and was ranked by The Wall Street Journal as one of the wealthiest, independent businessmen in Vietnam.

JACCAR Capital Fund invested into HAGL in 2006. From then on, JACCAR has been HAGL’s most active investors. Board members of both entities share their business experience and contribute professional support to reach optimal strategies.

HAGL’s future will reach far beyond furniture and real estate – developing a new frontier of renewable resources involving rubber, hydropower and sugarcane – set to emerge as a multi-million dollar business by the end of 2012.

APMG

Asia Pacific Medical Group (APMG) is a leading medical service provider founded by Michael Choy, a US-trained surgeon, and a group of US-based physicians and surgeons in 1992, which owns 10 hospital facilities in China. APMG’s hospitals and clinics offer a range of primary care and specialty services, with focuses including neurology, oncology, cardiovascular, maternity, pediatrics and dermatology. The hospital facilities are located in Beijing, Shanghai and Tianjin.

The Company also recently entered a joint venture agreement with a healthcare clinic chain with operations in Singapore, Malaysia and Hong Kong. APMG aims to help Chinese patients to access quality specialty healthcare services.
