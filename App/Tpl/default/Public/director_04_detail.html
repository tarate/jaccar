RENO MAURIZIO TONELLI

Born January 12, 1955

Italian Nationality


Mr. Reno Maurizo Tonelli graduated from Faculté de Droit of Nancy, France with a Masters degree in European and International Law. Between 1992 and 1996, he served as Head of International Credit Department at Société Européenne de Banque, Luxembourg, Head of Investment Fund Department at San Paolo Bank S.A., Luxembourg, and Head of Corporate Department (OPC-Credits- Holding companies) at San Paolo Bank S.A. Reno has been a partner of BDO since 2001 and a Board Member of JACCAR since 2010.
