GRAHAM JOHN PORTER

Born March 29, 1970

British Nationality


Mr. Graham John Porter graduated in 1992 from University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada with a business degree focused in transportation and logistics as well as accounting. Today, he is the Chairman of Hong Kong-based Tiger Group Investments, a sponsor of several shipping industry businesses including NYSE-listed Seaspan Corporation, Shanghai-based Greathorse Shipping Holdings Limited and SeaTiger Capital (an alternative provider of debt and equity capital to the energy and transportation markets). He is also a Board Member of Seaspan and Greater China Industrial Investments, an investment company established between the Carlyle Group and Tiger Group Investments. Mr. Graham John Porter has been a JACCAR Board Member since 2011.
