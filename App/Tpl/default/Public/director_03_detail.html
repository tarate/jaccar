JEAN-LOUIS WAUCQUEZ

Born February 9, 1969

Belgian Nationality


Mr. Jean-Louis Waucquez earned his Law degree and MBA from the Université Catholique de Louvain, Belgium. In 1994, he joined Banque Degroof, the main private bank in Belgium where he is now a manager. By 1995 he had successfully created a business unit to better service the Family Business Owner for clients of Banque Degroof, Luxembourg. At present, he is working on a new corporate strategy for UHNWI along with a strong network of professional advisors to better solve the most complex banking challenges across Europe. He was then appointed Head of the International Estate Planning Department.
