PIERRE LENTZ

Born April 22, 1959

Luxembourg Nationality


Mr. Pierre Lentz earned his Masters degree in Economics from the Free University of Bruxelles, Belgium in 1982. In 1985 he joined the National Institute of Independent Auditors (Institut des Réviseurs d’Entreprises) as well as the National Association of Chartered Accountants (Ordre des Experts Compatables Luxembourgeois) the latter of which he has been an advisor for since 2009. From 1982 to 1991, he audited commercial and industrial companies as well as banks for Compagnie Fiduciaire. Since 1991, he has specialized in international business, specifically the financial, commercial, industrial and real-estate service-sectors, joining the Board of Directors for numerous companies along the way. In 1999 Mr. Pierre Lentz became a partner of CF Corporate (formerly Compagnie Fiduciaire) and has been a Member of the Board of Directors of JACCAR since 2004.


OTHER FUNCTIONS
