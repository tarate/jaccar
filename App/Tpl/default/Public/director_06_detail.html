YUAN DING

Born October 30, 1969

French Nationality


Dr. Yuan Ding received his PhD in Accounting from the Institute of Enterprises Administration at the University Montesquieu Bordeaux IV, France. He also holds a Master's Degree in Enterprises Administration from the University of Poitiers, France. Prior to joining CEIBS as a Professor of Accounting, he was a tenured faculty member of HEC School of Management, Paris. As an avid publisher, he is editor and editorial board member of international academic journals in the field. As a lecturer on financial accounting, statement analysis, international accounting, corporate governance and M&A at the MS, MBA, EMBA and PhD levels, he also delivers special in-company programs in Europe and in China. In May 2011, Prof. Ding launched “Ding Yuan Index Neutral Fund” in A share market and became the first accounting and finance professor in mainland China running a hedge fund. In 2013, his fund received the Best Hedge Fund in China in the category of stable investment return. Mr. Yuan Ding has been a JACCAR Board Member since 2011.
